Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if a game is cancelled?

+

Cancelled or postponed games are removed from the contest. Your score is calculated based on the remaining games, and all players are scored fairly.

When do I receive my winnings?

+

Winnings are paid out automatically within 24 hours after all contest games are final. Payments go directly to your connected XUMM wallet.

Can I change my picks after submitting?

+

No, picks are final once submitted. Make sure you're confident in your selections before confirming your entry.

What sports are included?

+

We include all major professional sports: NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, Soccer, and more. Games are selected based on availability and betting interest.

Do I need to be in a specific country?

+

Our platform is available globally, but please check your local laws regarding online contests and cryptocurrency use.
